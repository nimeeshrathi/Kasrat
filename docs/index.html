What it does

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Built for lifters who want to log fast and see real progress — not wrestle with sign-ups, ads, or a hundred settings.

🏋️

Log workouts fast

Track weight × reps, bodyweight, time and distance. Per-set traffic lights show when you beat your last session.

📋

Routines & schedule

Build your own routines and pin them to weekdays. Start a planned session in one tap from the home screen.

⏱️

Rest timer that buzzes

An auto-starting rest clock that vibrates and notifies you when it's time for the next set — even with the phone locked.

📈

Strength progress

Estimated 1RM trend lines per exercise, automatic personal records, and a muscle-balance breakdown.

⚖️

BMI tracking

Add your height & weight and plot your BMI over time against the healthy range — updated whenever you log a new weight.

🔥

Streaks & badges

Weekly workout targets, training streaks, and unlockable badges keep the momentum going.

🔄

Share routines

Export your data to a file and import a friend's routines — merged in safely, without touching your own.

🔒

Private & offline

No login, no servers, no tracking. Everything is stored on your device; you own a one-tap backup file.

Get it on your phone

Install in three steps

It's a debug build for personal use, so Android asks you to confirm an install from outside the Play Store. That's expected.

1

Download the APK

Tap Download APK on your Android phone (or transfer the file to it).

2

Allow the install

Open the file. If prompted, allow your browser or Files app to install unknown apps.

3

Open Kasrat

Tap Install, then launch Kasrat from your app drawer and start logging.

⬇ Download Kasrat.apk

Requires Android 7.0 or newer. The app never connects to a server — if you ever switch phones, use Export data in the app to carry your routines and history across.
